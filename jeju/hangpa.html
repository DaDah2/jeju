Hangpaduri

Introduction

Historic Site of Anti-Mongolian Struggle in Hangpadu-ri is where the
Sambyeolcho military unit of the Goryeo dynasty resisted the invasion
of Mongolia until the last possible moment. The site houses an
exhibition hall, a monument, and a unique mud fortress. Nearby is
Jangsumul Spring (Jangsu means general) which flowed from the
footprints of General Kim Tong-jeong. Today, water from the spring
cannot be used as drinking water.
